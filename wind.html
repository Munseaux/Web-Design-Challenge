Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Our planet typically has very low wind speeds when compared to other planets with atmospheres. We benefit from this. Most of the wind speeds on this chart hover around the 0-5mph level. Winds of 30-40mph can cause large amounts of damage and should be avoided. Luckily, most of the cities in this dataset manage to keep their wind speed levels relatively low.


There is, again, very little correlation in this data. The highest wind speeds also have very high latitude, so perhaps there is a relationship there.
