Summary: Latitude vs. Temperature

Temperature is highly correlated with latitude, and it appears to have an exponential relationship. This is to be expected, as temperature tend to decrease the further one gets from the equator. This phenomenon has been widely documented, and is generally accepted both in the scientific community, and among the general public.


As you can see from the graph, the data points are represented by blue dots, whereas the points on the graph that do not contain data are represented by the absence of blue dots.
